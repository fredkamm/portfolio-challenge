About Me

My name is Fred Kamm and I am a Web Developer. I live in Bucks County, Pennsylvania, which is about an hour away from the city of Philadelphia. I graduated from East Stroudsburg University of Pennsylvania in 2017 where I earned my bachelor's degree in business management. I first started coding in the Spring of 2020 when Covid-19 first hit. The pandemic caused a massive work shortage in my previous line of work so I decided to make a career change and learn how to code. I chose to enroll in a coding Bootcamp that the University of Pennsylvania offers. It is here where I really started to develop into an advanced web developer.
